Why hiking?

Hiking is a good reset because it combines movement, planning, and a change of scenery. A good trail day does not have to be extreme; it just needs to match the weather, time available, and group energy.

Planning checklist with nested lists

  1. Choose the trail difficulty.
    • Easy: short trail, low elevation gain.
    • Moderate: longer trail with steady climbing.
    • Hard: long distance, steep sections, or rough terrain.
  2. Pack the basics.
    • Water and snacks
    • Map or downloaded directions
    • Light jacket and sunscreen
  3. Check the return plan before leaving.

Weather

Check temperature, wind, and storm risk before choosing a trail.

Time

Leave enough daylight for the hike, breaks, photos, and the drive home.

Safety

Tell someone where you are going and when you expect to return.
